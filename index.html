The William Druhan Jr. Scholarship Awards  

The Albert Einstein High School (AEHS) Athletic Hall of Fame (HOF) and the Family of William T. (Billy) Druhan, Jr. established the William Druhan Scholarship Awards for the Outstanding Female and Male AEHS Soccer Players in 2012. Billy was born on July 4th and we honor his memory and legacy by designating each July as fund raising month for the Druhan Awards.

Billy was a member of the AEHS Class of 1965 and was inducted into the HOF in 2006 for his accomplishments in soccer, football and track. Billy was the captain of Einstein's first soccer team and received the first letter ever awarded for soccer. We lost Billy to pancreatic cancer in June of 2011 and in 2012 the first scholarships for soccer were awarded to AEHS students in his name.

The Hall of Fame awards 2 - $2,500 scholarships in Billy's name each year; one each to the outstanding female and male soccer players. To-date, the Hall of Fame has awarded 13 Druhan scholarships for a total of $29,500. The fundraising goal for this July is $7,500. The success of this annual fundraising effort will help assure that the Druhan Awards for soccer excellence will continue to encourage and reward AEHS students who strive to meet the high standards that Billy set during his years at AEHS.
